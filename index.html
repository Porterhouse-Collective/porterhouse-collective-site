Startup Hiring Strategy

01

Designing the right hiring roadmap for early-stage and scaling companies. This includes role prioritization, hiring sequencing, budget planning, and defining what great looks like for each role so teams can hire intentionally instead of reactively.

Recruiting Process & Hiring System Setup

02

Building structured recruiting systems that make hiring faster, clearer, and more consistent. This includes interview frameworks, candidate evaluation processes, scorecards, and practical workflows that allow teams to scale hiring without chaos.

Marketplace & Network Development

03

Designing and growing talent networks, referral ecosystems, and marketplace-style sourcing strategies. Helping organizations build sustainable pipelines of high-quality candidates through relationships, communities, and structured outreach.

Operational Process Review & Improvement

04

Assessing existing operational workflows and identifying where systems, communication, or decision making break down. Delivering practical improvements that increase clarity, reduce friction, and help teams execute more effectively.
